Privacy Policy

Hangman Mobile App does not collect, store, or share any personal user data. There is no authentication, subscription, or account creation required to use the app.

The only data stored is your high score, which is saved locally on your device using iOS Core Data. This information is not shared with anyone and remains private to your device.

The app displays rewarded ads provided by Google. These ads may appear after you win four consecutive games. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for details on how ads are managed. Google may collect certain information as described in their privacy policy.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, feel free to contact us at e4eozcelik@gmail.com.
